<p>
  We reached Gama at around 3:30 and found the albergue - a small detached building near a bar (from where we retrieved the key) adjacent to the Fev&eacute; metre-gauge railway line that runs along the north coast. We were the only guests at the albergue; the last pilgrims were here over a week ago. 
</p>

<p>
  The normal hiking stage for the day ended in Laredo but we're trying to make up a bit of time - even if our mileage doesn't indicate that! We're hoping to avoid Santander - our guide suggests a train out of Santander to avoid the traffic, not painting an inviting picture - so we may skirt southward to give the city a miss altogether.
</p>

<Img
  src="/images/2015/07/2015-07-21-074332.jpg"
   alt="21/7/2015"
/>

<p>It had been a hot, tiring and somewhat uninspiring day. A local came round in the evening to collect our fees, then we snacked for dinner and climbed into our bunks for a warm night.</p>
